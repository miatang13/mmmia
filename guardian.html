Problem Space

Waste Management @ Pittsburgh

The problem of waste management is exacerbated by the increasing population density, and is often seen as a "black-box" due to its complicated nature.

Understanding Through Mapping

Future Projection Map / Mass Map / Systems Map

Initial Mappings:
Collective Brainstorming

We communicated and merged our ideas through physical post-it and venn-diagrams to show our multi-perspective understanding of waste management.

Secondary Mapping:
Digital Collaboration

As our understanding furthered and idea developed, we switched to using an online platform to work toward our final digital product.

Final Mapping

To present our thorough understanding of the existing system, we divided information into 6 categories based on the waste's original location.


The Systems Map was exhibited at RSD8 Systems Thinking Conference.
