Guardian

Collaborators: Joseph Zhang, Youie Cho, Sammie Kim, Elizabeth Han, Charmaine Qiu

Duration: 3 Weeks
My Role: Research, Systems Mapping, Information Architecture, UI / UX Design

As a group, my teammates and I dived into the wicked problem, Waste Management in Pittsburgh, and proposed a digital intervention to solve the issue.

Big Ideas /

Part A
The problem of waste management is exacerbated by the increasing population density, and is often seen as a "blackbox" due to its complicated nature. To understand the problem, my team and I conducted in-depth research into the various aspect of waste-disposal.

Part B
After understanding the problem, I designed a digital intervention (UI / UX), the Guardian application, as a potential enhancement to the existing waste management system.

Initial Mappings: Collective Brainstorming

At the beginning phase of the project, we engaged in face-to-face discussions with our team members, collaboratively came to a thorough and multi-perspective understanding of the waste management system in the local Pittsburgh area. We communicated and merged our ideas through physical post-it and venn-diagrams to work as a team efficiently.






Mapping using Kumu

Secondary Mapping: Digital Collaboration

As our understanding furthered and idea developed, we switched to using an online platform to work toward our final digital product. In this project, we used Kumu to layer complex information in top of our structure mapping.

Final Mapping

To present our thorough understanding of the existing system, we divided information into 6 categories based on the waste's original location.


The Systems Map was exhibited at RSD8 Systems Thinking Conference.

Part B /
Designing Intervention: Garbage Guardian

SOLO PROJECT: Service Design / UI UX

I started with asking myself questions:
What's the one place that everyone has to go to? What's the most crucial artifact of everyday waste disposal experience?

My answer was: The garbage cans.

This inspired me to design a system of interventions that are digital, physical, and hybrid.

Overall User Flow

This diagram maps the entire experience from physical to digital through connecting tangible artifact, the smart garbage can, with the digital app.
